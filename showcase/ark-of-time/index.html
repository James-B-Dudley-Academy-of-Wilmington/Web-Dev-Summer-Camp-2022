Ark of time

made by Archie

Im thinking of a 32 bit sorta skyrim like game.
Im going to add some boss fights with a diablo like looting system i am also going to make it a 3rd person camera view.
there is going to be magic and weapons.


The weapon system is going to make it so that you can loot bosses for weapons and armor and maybe some potions.
you can also by them from a trader or the market and if you have the right materials you can even make weapons!
you can sharpen worn out weapons and even enchanted them!


Some of the boss fights will not be open to you if you are not a high enough level.
Their is also going to be a mutiplayer which you can join with friends.
you can also play local mutiplayer if you want to play with your friends in a group to fight bosses together.
